    Favorite Animal

    DUCKS!!!

    Did you know:

    • Ducks are highly adaptable birds and can be found on every continent except Antarctica.
    • There are over 120 different species of ducks, each with its own unique characteristics.
    • Male ducks are called drakes, females are hens, and ducklings are the young ones.
    • Ducks have waterproof feathers due to a special gland near their tail that produces oil.
    • They are excellent swimmers and can paddle through water with their webbed feet.
    • Ducks are omnivores, eating a variety of foods such as insects, small fish, plants, and grains.
    • Some duck species are known for their long migrations, covering thousands of miles.
    • Their quacks have different sounds and pitches, varying among species and individuals.
    • Female ducks are known for their elaborate quacking when they are leading their ducklings.
    • Several duck species are kept as domesticated animals for their eggs, meat, and feathers.
