Favorite Show(s)

Love Island

Love Island originated as a British dating game show. Since its creation, there's been over 20 spinoff shows ranging from countries like Spain to Austrailia. While the show does feature a plethora of hot bods and gravure figures, the central theme of the show is to look past the outer appearance and find the deeper meaning behind personality, soul, and values to attempt a relationship that is more than skin-deep to win the show's large prize-pool!

Too Hot to Handle

The premise behind Too Hot to Handle is very similar to Love Island:

Contestants value physical relationships above all else, so the motivation to help them create deeper connections come in the form of (1) rules barring any/all physical contact and (2) a prize pool that dips anytime violations of the rules occurs.
