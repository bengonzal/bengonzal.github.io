Scenario:

Congratulations! You just won the lottery and never have to worry about finances ever again.
Some time has passed and you've exhausted all avenues of entertainment. You must now decide to work odd jobs that would have otherwise been unsustainable.

What do you do for work?

Fortune Cookie Writer

"Go to bed with itchy butt, wake up with stinky fingers."

I definitely think this could be entertaining for a while, only if I were able to somehow cosmically see the peoples' reactions when they open my fortune cookies. I'd like to think I could scare some of them with eerie fortunes like...

Don't turn around, they don't like when you look at them.

Tonight, a whispered harmony will accompany your lullabies, bringing an unexpected guest to your child's dreams.

It won't matter if you lock up tonight. I'm on my way.

We'd like to reach you about your car's extended warranty.

Professional Sleeper

This is something I could do with my eyes closed.

Okay so apparently this might actually be a real occupation. I guess mattress companies have quality control people they hire to sleep on and test their mattresses for comfort. I bet you I could do this for at least a couple of weeks without any issues.

I'd test out a couple of things with each mattress like...

Sleeping position quality

If I sleep backwards, do I get the same quality of sleep?

Crumb retention factor

Say I want to eat saltine crackers, doritos, sour cream and cheddar ruffles in the bed...how much leftover crumbs remain when I go to sleep, and will they affect my quality of sleep?

Trampoline substitution

How many monkeys may jump on the bed before one falls off and bumps its head?

Professional Ghost Hunter

I think that ghost just said it wants me to take another shot of whiskey...

This would be a fun one I think. I don't know if I believe in ghosts entirely, but I would like to hope that they're real. If ghosts are simply the spirits of humans who haven't moved on to the other side...wouldn't they be somewhat harmless?

I'd like the thrill of being in the dark and calling out to see if something responds. It would get the heart racing! Also, if you've ever seen Ghost Adventures then you'd know it gets pretty silly, so that makes me think it'd be fun if I could do it with friends.

Another important question: If I soaked some gloves in holy water, do you think I could punch a ghost in its dumb little ghost face?

Sorry, that was a strange thing to ask.
